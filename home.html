GettingCALIBRATED

To get started you will have to go through a brief process that is explained below. This will not only help us become more personalized for you, but also would allow you to breath calmly instead of being rushed or bored because of the horrible pace. To begin, follow the steps below:
  1. Take a deep breath

  2. The moment you do, press the play button

  3. When you exhale press the pause button

  4. Repeat steps 1 - 3 a total of five times
